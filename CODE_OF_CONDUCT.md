# BrainVION Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

BrainVION is a student technology community dedicated to learning, collaboration, and mutual respect. 

We aim to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

All participants in the BrainVION community are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

---

## Expected Behavior

Members of the BrainVION community are expected to:

• Be respectful and supportive of others  
• Encourage learning and knowledge sharing  
• Provide constructive feedback  
• Maintain professional and positive communication  
• Help create a safe and friendly environment  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

• Harassment or discrimination of any kind  
• Personal attacks or insulting language  
• Trolling or disruptive behavior  
• Publishing private information without permission  
• Any conduct that harms the community environment  

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the responsibility to enforce this Code of Conduct.

If a participant violates these rules, maintainers may take actions including:

• Warning the contributor  
• Removing comments or contributions  
• Temporarily or permanently restricting access  

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainers.

Contact: brainvion.community@gmail.com

All reports will be reviewed and handled respectfully and confidentially.

---

BrainVION is committed to building a supportive and positive student tech community.
